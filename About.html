About WebMinds

Wellcome to WebMinds, you go-to destination for all things tech,coding and web development. WebMinds is a vertically integrated Internet Media company which publishes and manages Web sites that reach IT professionals, small and home based businesses, individual PC Users and executives in all phases of the technology decision-making and purchase process.

Through its industry-leading Web sites and the deployment of its award winning web 2.0 applications, WebMinds delivers services, products and information to maximize the use of a PC and the usefulness of the Internet for all users.

WebMinds recognizes the importance of privacy in today's interconnected world. Their product "FileCleaner" addresses this concern by allowing users to securely delete sensitive files, ensuring that no traces are left behind that could potentially be exploited.
